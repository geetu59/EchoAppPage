How To Perform Echo Setup

It’s a breeze to perform an Echo setup, provided, you know how to do it correctly. Still, if you find yourself stuck anywhere during the setup process or even while using it, you can take the supportive hand of our experts to get you easy solutions. Let’s see how you can set up your device on your own:

  • Unbox your Echo device and get everything necessary handy, such as adapter and your smartphone or tablet with which you’re going to configure it
  • On your phone or tablet, download and install the Echo app
  • Plug your Echo device in a power outlet with the help of the adapter
  • Turn on both the power source and your Echo device and wait while the light ring atop turns on completely. First, it will glow blue and then orange
  • When the speaker greets, it means you can move to the next step
  • Launch the Echo app in your phone or tablet and head to its settings
  • Tap on “set up a new device” to establish a connection with your WiFi
  • Select your Echo device from the device list
  • On the next page, tap on “Connect to Wi-Fi”
  • Make sure your Echo device has an orange light ring turned on. If not, you can press the Action button and put the device into the setup mode
  • Now, set up your voice with the device to familiarize it with your voice type, tone, and accents. You can also change the wake word

That’s it! You can now use the Echo to get your daily tasks done in an easy and fast manner.
